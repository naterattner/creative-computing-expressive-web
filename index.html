Star Spectrum

Our digital world yields remarkable power on its physical counterpart. Ratings on review websites such as Yelp influence where we eat, shop, play, and call for help.

Beneath these aggregate star ratings often lies a range of personal experiences, with glowing five-star reviews left alongside scathing one-stars. Personal experiences can vary dramatically, and online platforms can bring out extreme viewpoints. But these digital reviews can shape a reader's perception of a place and sway how they spend their physical time. In many cases, that decision can be made simply by which review a reader sees first.

This web installation displays an analysis of a Yelp dataset covering more than 150,000 businesses and 6.9 million reviews. It includes businesses that received both five and one-star reviews within the most recent year of the dataset, from early 2021 through early 2022.

Share of reviews on Yelp

  • Four 21%
  • Three 10%
  • Two 8%
